Tool 1 — Hiring License

What the Hiring License does

It gives hiring managers a structured, sales-specific read on every candidate before an offer is made, and plugs into the interview process you already run. For context: 33% of candidates an OMG assessment does not recommend go on to fail within their first year.

Predictive

4× more predictive than behavioral or personality tests. 92% of OMG-recommended candidates reach the top half of the sales force.

Sales-specific

Built only for selling roles. Measures Will to Sell, Sales DNA, and tactical selling — the things that actually move revenue.

Role-specific

Configured to your selling environment — hunters vs. farmers, inside vs. field, named accounts, verticals and territories.

Actionable

Every report says Recommended or Not Recommended for the role, with the specific competencies behind the call.

Customizable

Tune the assessment to mirror the traits of your current top performers and EEOC-compliant by design.

Fits your workflow

Integrates with your existing ATS and interview process. Results back in about 5 minutes after a candidate finishes.

1

Tool 2 — Sales Team Evaluation

What the Team Evaluation does

The SEIA gives individual and team-level diagnostics of the sales force you already have: who has room to grow, who may be in the wrong seat, where the sales process breaks down, and what to coach next — so decisions rest on data rather than instinct.

Hidden potential

Find reps who are underperforming because of fixable issues — not lack of ability — and the ceiling on each person.

Process reality

Do they follow a sales process, or wing it? See where deals stall and where to build accountability.

What drives each rep

Uncover real motivators so leaders can incentivize, develop and retain people more effectively.

How an engagement runs

1

Evaluate

2

3

4

Checkpoint
